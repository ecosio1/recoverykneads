Serving the Estero Community

Estero is one of Southwest Florida's fastest-growing communities, blending suburban comfort with easy access to shopping, dining, and outdoor recreation. At Recovery Kneads, we understand the diverse wellness needs of Estero residents - from busy professionals and FGCU students to active retirees enjoying the Florida lifestyle.

Our massage therapy services address the needs of Estero's dynamic community:

  • Work-related stress and computer posture issues
  • Golf injuries from courses like The Club at Grandezza and Estero Country Club
  • Athletic recovery for FGCU students and recreational athletes
  • Shopping fatigue from Coconut Point and Miromar Outlets
  • Cycling and running injuries from Estero Community Park trails
  • General wellness and stress management

Located in North Naples, just 15-18 minutes south on US-41, Recovery Kneads is an easy drive from anywhere in Estero. Many of our Estero clients combine their massage appointments with trips to Naples for shopping, dining, or other errands, making it a convenient addition to their regular routine.
