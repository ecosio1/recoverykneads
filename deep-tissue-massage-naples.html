What is Deep Tissue Massage?

Deep tissue massage is a therapeutic technique that focuses on realigning deeper layers of muscles and connective tissue. It's especially helpful for chronic aches and pain, contracted areas such as stiff neck and upper back, low back pain, leg muscle tightness, and sore shoulders.

Your Session

What to Expect During Deep Tissue Massage

1

Initial Consultation (5 minutes)

We'll discuss your pain history, problem areas, and goals for treatment. This helps us create the most effective treatment plan.

2

Assessment & Preparation (5 minutes)

Brief physical assessment of posture, range of motion, and tension patterns. You'll undress to your comfort level and get situated on the massage table.

3

Warm-Up Phase (10 minutes)

We start with lighter pressure to warm the muscles and prepare them for deeper work. This prevents injury and increases effectiveness.

4

Deep Tissue Work (30 minutes)

Focused work on problem areas using various techniques. You may feel intense pressure, but it should never be unbearably painful. Communication is key!

5

Integration & Aftercare (5 minutes)

Light movements to help your body integrate the work. We'll discuss home care recommendations and schedule follow-up if needed.

Important Things to Know

🗣️ Communicate Freely

Always tell us if pressure is too much or if you need adjustments. Your comfort is paramount.

💧 Expect Some Soreness

Mild soreness for 24-48 hours is normal. Drink plenty of water and use ice if needed.

⏱️ Results Take Time

Chronic conditions often require multiple sessions for best results. We'll discuss a treatment plan.

🏃 Stay Active

Gentle movement after your session helps maintain the benefits and prevents stiffness.
