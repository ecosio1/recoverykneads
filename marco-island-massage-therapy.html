Serving the Marco Island Community

Marco Island's unique island lifestyle brings its own set of wellness needs. Whether you're a year-round resident or seasonal visitor, the activities that make Marco Island special - boating, fishing, beach combing, and water sports - can take a toll on your body. Recovery Kneads understands these unique needs and provides targeted therapeutic care.

Our massage therapy services are perfect for addressing:

  • Muscle strain from deep-sea fishing and boating
  • Back and shoulder pain from golf at Island Country Club or Hideaway Beach
  • Tension from long beach walks on Tigertail or South Marco Beach
  • Sports injuries from tennis, pickleball, and beach volleyball
  • General fatigue from entertaining guests and vacation activities
  • Stress relief for both residents and visitors

While the drive from Marco Island takes about 25-30 minutes, many of our Marco Island clients consider it a worthwhile journey for professional therapeutic care. Some combine their massage appointments with shopping trips to North Naples or other errands, making it a productive wellness day off the island.
