First-Time Clients

Your first visit will include:

  1. Arrival: Please arrive 10 minutes early
  2. Intake Forms: Brief health history and goals
  3. Consultation: Discussion of your needs and any concerns
  4. Treatment Plan: Customized approach based on your input
  5. Massage Session: 45 minutes of therapeutic care
  6. After-Care: Recommendations for ongoing wellness

Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that's easy to remove and put back on. You'll undress to your comfort level and be properly draped with sheets and towels throughout the session.

Most clients undress completely for optimal treatment, but you should only do what makes you comfortable. Your privacy and comfort are always respected.

Yes, absolutely. Professional draping is maintained at all times. Only the area being worked on is uncovered, and you're properly covered with clean sheets and towels throughout the entire session.

Your privacy, comfort, and dignity are our highest priorities.

Massage should never be painful. You may experience:

  • "Good hurt": Pressure that feels intense but therapeutic
  • Muscle release: Sensation of tension melting away
  • Increased circulation: Warmth and tingling

We start gently and adjust based on your feedback. Always communicate if anything feels uncomfortable - we can modify pressure, technique, or focus areas immediately.

Please inform us of any:

  • Recent injuries or surgeries
  • Chronic conditions (arthritis, fibromyalgia, etc.)
  • High blood pressure or heart conditions
  • Pregnancy
  • Allergies or skin sensitivities
  • Areas you prefer we avoid
  • Current medications that affect circulation

This information helps us provide safe, effective treatment tailored to your needs.
