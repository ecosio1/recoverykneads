Serving the Pelican Bay Community

Pelican Bay is one of Naples' premier communities, known for its beautiful beaches, world-class golf courses, and active lifestyle. At Recovery Kneads, we understand the unique needs of Pelican Bay residents who enjoy golf at the Club Pelican Bay, tennis at the community courts, and walks along the pristine beaches.

Our massage therapy services are designed to support your active lifestyle, whether you're dealing with:

  • Golf-related muscle tension and back pain
  • Tennis elbow and shoulder strain
  • General stress from daily activities
  • Chronic pain from repetitive movements
  • Recovery needs from beach activities and water sports

Located just north of Pelican Bay on Tamiami Trail, Recovery Kneads provides a peaceful, professional environment where you can receive the therapeutic care you deserve. Our proximity to Pelican Bay means you can easily fit a massage session into your day without the hassle of traveling to downtown Naples or further south.
