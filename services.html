Specialized Techniques

Massage Modalities & Techniques

Each massage session is customized to your needs. Depending on your goals, I may incorporate one or more of the following:

MediCupping (Vacuum Cupping Therapy)

A negative-pressure therapy that uses controlled suction to gently lift tissue. This technique helps improve blood and lymphatic circulation, reduce fascial adhesions, and decrease musculoskeletal pain associated with restricted soft tissue.

MPS Therapy (Dolphin Neurostim)

Microcurrent Point Stimulation (MPS) applies low-level electrical impulses to acupuncture and trigger points using the Dolphin Neurostim device. This device is specifically designed for the relief of muscular pain due to injuries and everyday strains or overuse.

Gua Sha (Scraping Therapy)

A traditional manual therapy using specialized tools to scrape along the skin's surface. Gua sha promotes increased circulation, reduces stagnation, and assists in breaking down fascial restrictions, supporting mobility and recovery from chronic tension patterns.
