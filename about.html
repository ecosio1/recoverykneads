Our Story

Founded on a Passion for Healing

Recovery Kneads was born from a deep understanding that chronic pain and stress don't have to be a way of life. After witnessing countless individuals struggle with pain that affected their work, relationships, and daily joy, we knew there had to be a better way.

Located in Naples, Florida, Recovery Kneads has become the most trusted massage therapy practice serving Naples FL, North Naples, Old Naples, and surrounding areas. We've built our practice on the foundation that therapeutic massage is not a luxury, but an essential component of health and wellness.

What sets us apart is our commitment to truly understanding each client's unique situation. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all treatments. Instead, we take time to listen, assess, and create customized treatment plans that address not just your symptoms, but the root causes of your discomfort.
