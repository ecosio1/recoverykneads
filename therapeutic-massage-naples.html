What is Therapeutic Massage?

Therapeutic massage is a personalized combination of techniques tailored to alleviate physical tension and promote relaxation. The applied pressure is customized to individual comfort levels, ensuring its efficacy in managing stress, enhancing circulation, and alleviating general discomfort and stiffness.

Unlike more intensive massage styles, therapeutic massage uses flowing, nurturing strokes that work with your body's natural healing processes. It's perfect for anyone seeking stress relief, better sleep, improved circulation, and overall wellness enhancement.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about therapeutic massage and what to expect during your visit.

What should I expect during my first therapeutic massage?

Your first visit includes a brief consultation about your wellness goals and stress levels. The massage itself uses gentle, flowing techniques to promote relaxation. You'll leave feeling deeply relaxed and refreshed.

How is therapeutic massage different from deep tissue massage?

Therapeutic massage uses gentler pressure and focuses on overall relaxation and stress relief. It's perfect if you prefer a nurturing, calming experience rather than intense pressure.

What should I wear to my appointment?

Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothes. You'll undress to your comfort level before the massage - most clients undress completely, but you can keep on whatever makes you comfortable.

How often should I get therapeutic massage?

For stress relief and general wellness, once every 2-4 weeks is beneficial. During high-stress periods, weekly sessions can provide extra support. We'll help you determine the best frequency for your needs.

Can I get therapeutic massage if I'm pregnant?

Yes! Therapeutic massage can be wonderfully beneficial during pregnancy. Please let us know when booking so we can use appropriate techniques and positioning for your comfort and safety.

What if I fall asleep during the massage?

Falling asleep is completely normal and actually a sign that you're deeply relaxed! Many clients drift off during their session. We'll gently wake you when it's time.
