Common Questions

Before You Contact Us

How do I book an appointment?

You can book online through our scheduler, call (239) 427-4757, or send us a message. We often have same-day availability.

What should I expect for my first visit?

Arrive 10 minutes early to complete intake forms. We'll discuss your needs and customize your treatment accordingly.

Do you accept insurance or HSA/FSA?

Yes! We accept HSA/FSA accounts and work with select insurance plans. Contact us to verify your coverage.

What's your cancellation policy?

We have a flexible 24-hour cancellation policy. Life happens, and we understand that sometimes you need to reschedule.
