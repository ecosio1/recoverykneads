Safe Prenatal Massage Naples FL - Your Peace of Mind

As a licensed massage therapist in Naples FL, I understand the unique needs and safety considerations for prenatal massage. Every session is carefully adapted for your stage of pregnancy.

Safety Measures Include:

  • Specialized prenatal massage table and positioning
  • Avoidance of pressure points that could trigger contractions
  • Modified techniques for each trimester
  • Temperature and comfort considerations
  • Continuous communication about your comfort level
  • Coordination with your healthcare provider when needed

Important: Prenatal massage is generally safe after the first trimester. Please consult with your healthcare provider before scheduling, and inform us of any high-risk conditions or concerns.

Prenatal Massage FAQ - Naples FL

When is it safe to start prenatal massage?

Prenatal massage is generally considered safe after the first trimester (12+ weeks). However, we recommend consulting with your healthcare provider before your first session, especially if you have any high-risk conditions.

What positions are used during prenatal massage?

We use specialized prenatal massage positioning including side-lying with supportive pillows and semi-reclining positions. We avoid lying flat on your back after 20 weeks and ensure your comfort throughout the session.

How often should I get prenatal massage?

Many women benefit from monthly prenatal massage sessions, with some choosing bi-weekly sessions during the third trimester. Frequency depends on your individual needs, comfort level, and healthcare provider's recommendations.

Do you offer postnatal massage in Naples FL?

Yes! We offer postnatal massage to support your recovery after childbirth. This includes lymphatic drainage, stress relief, posture correction, and overall wellness support during your postpartum journey.
