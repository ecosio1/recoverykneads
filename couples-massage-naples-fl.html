Couples Massage FAQ - Naples FL

How much does couples massage cost in Naples FL?

Our couples massage starts at $180 for two 45-minute sessions. This includes side-by-side massage tables and personalized treatment for both partners. Special occasion packages range from $190-$200.

Do we need to book in advance for couples massage?

Yes, we recommend booking couples massage 3-7 days in advance, especially for weekends and special occasions like Valentine's Day or anniversaries. This ensures availability and proper preparation.

What should we bring to couples massage?

Just bring yourselves! We provide everything needed including robes, towels, and comfort amenities. Arrive in comfortable clothing and prepare to relax together.

Can we talk during couples massage?

While some couples enjoy quiet conversation during their massage, we encourage focusing on relaxation for the best therapeutic benefits. You can communicate with your therapists about pressure and comfort as needed.
