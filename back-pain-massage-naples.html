Back Pain Massage FAQ - Naples FL

How effective is massage therapy for back pain?

Clinical studies show massage therapy can be highly effective for both acute and chronic back pain. Most clients experience significant pain reduction within 2-4 sessions, with continued improvement over time.

How often should I get massage for chronic back pain?

For chronic pain, we typically recommend starting with weekly sessions for 4-6 weeks, then transitioning to bi-weekly or monthly maintenance sessions based on your response and needs.

Is massage therapy safe for herniated discs?

Massage can be beneficial for disc-related pain, but requires specialized techniques and medical clearance. We work within your comfort level and may coordinate with your healthcare provider for optimal safety.

Do you accept insurance for back pain massage in Naples?

Yes, we work with select insurance plans. Many plans cover massage therapy when prescribed by a physician for pain management. Contact us to verify your coverage.
