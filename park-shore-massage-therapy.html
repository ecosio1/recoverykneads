Serving the Park Shore Community

Park Shore is one of Naples' most desirable beachfront communities, known for its pristine beaches, upscale shopping at Venetian Village, and beautiful waterfront properties. At Recovery Kneads, we understand the unique wellness needs of Park Shore residents who enjoy an active coastal lifestyle.

Our massage therapy services are designed to support your beachside activities, whether you're dealing with:

  • Muscle soreness from swimming and water sports
  • Tension from paddleboarding or kayaking
  • Beach volleyball or tennis-related strain
  • General fatigue from beach walks and outdoor activities
  • Stress relief from daily life

Located just minutes north of Park Shore on Tamiami Trail, Recovery Kneads offers a tranquil escape where you can receive professional therapeutic care. Our convenient location means you can easily schedule a massage session after shopping at Venetian Village or spending a day at Park Shore beach.
